## 6. Blocked Credit (ITC Not Allowed) â€” Important

ITC **cannot be claimed** on:

* Food & beverages (employee meals, restaurants, catering)
* Personal expenses
* Motor vehicles (unless logistics/taxi business)
* Travel benefits for employees
* Club memberships
* Health & life insurance for employees
* Goods/services used for personal consumption
* Outdoor catering services
* Health & fitness services
* Rent-a-cab services (unless specific business use)

Agent must flag these as:
"Non-creditable GST expense."

## 10. Reverse Charge Mechanism (RCM)

In certain cases, buyer must pay GST instead of seller.

Common triggers:

* Legal services (from individual advocates)
* Import of services
* Unregistered vendor purchases
* GTA (Goods Transport Agency) services
* Security services (manpower supply)
* Sponsorship services

Agent should flag unusual vendor cases.

---

## 12. GST Compliance Risks SMBs Commonly Miss

Agent should detect:

* Claiming ITC on blocked categories
* Not reconciling receivables with GST payable
* Revenue booked without tax provision
* Vendor GSTIN missing
* High expenses but low ITC utilization
* 180-day payment rule violation (ITC reversal needed)
* Mixing personal and business expenses

## 15. Common Expense Classifications

**Software/Cloud (18% GST, ITC Eligible):**
AWS, Azure, GCP, SaaS, hosting, domain, GitHub, Zoho, Microsoft 365, Adobe, Slack, Atlassian

**Professional Services (18% GST, ITC Eligible):**
Legal fees, CA services, consulting, audit, tax advisory, freelancer services (development, design, content)

**Office Rent (18% GST, ITC Eligible):**
Commercial property rent, co-working space, warehouse

**Utilities (18% GST, ITC Eligible):**
Electricity (commercial), internet, broadband, telephone, mobile

**Travel - Eligible (5% GST, ITC Eligible):**
Hotel accommodation, business flight/train tickets

**Travel - Blocked (No ITC):**
Taxi/cab (Uber, Ola), personal vehicle expenses

**Food - Blocked (No ITC):**
Restaurant bills, Swiggy, Zomato, catering, team meals, client dinners

**Capital Goods (18% GST, ITC Eligible):**
Laptops, computers, monitors, furniture, printers, machinery, equipment

**Maintenance (18% GST, ITC Eligible):**
Cleaning services, repairs, AMC, pest control

**Marketing (18% GST, ITC Eligible):**
Advertising, digital marketing, branding, promotional materials

**Banking (18% GST on some, Often Exempt):**
Payment gateway fees (ITC Eligible), bank charges (often exempt)

## 17. Risk Flags

**High Risk:**
- Claiming ITC on food/meals
- Missing vendor GSTIN on large purchases
- Revenue without GST provision

**Medium Risk:**
- High receivables ratio (>30% of revenue)
- Low ITC utilization despite eligible expenses
- Unclear expense descriptions

**Low Risk:**
- Well-documented transactions
- Clear business purpose
- Proper invoice compliance
